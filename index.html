Learning for Fun

We are a fun, youthful company that aims to teach math in a new, exciting way.
We have created a multiplication board game to play with family and friends at home and in the classroom.
What makes our game different is that we teach with a different strategy.
We show the multiplication answer on the game piece and the equation on the board and leave it up to the player to make different con-x-ions!
We have gotten two 10/10s and a 9/10.
